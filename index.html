Vegetables, Bees & Hydroponics

We combine traditional growing with modern techniques so visitors can compare methods, outcomes, and sustainability benefits in one place.

Vegetable Garden

  • Leafy greens, herbs, and local crops for seasonal harvesting and direct sales.
  • Learning on composting, soil health, and organic methods.
  • Community harvest days connecting food and wellbeing.

Bees & Honey Harvest

  • We rear bees carefully to support pollination and ecosystem health.
  • Seasonal honey harvest activities are part of our educational sessions.
  • Fresh Tempua Kebun honey is available for sale alongside our vegetables.

Hydroponic System

  • Vertical: stacked channels that maximize small footprints.
  • Horizontal: easy-access rows for routine maintenance.
  • Water-efficient, clean growing for urban-friendly farming.

    • Vegetables, Bees & Hydroponic Innovation

    Tempua Kebun combines traditional gardening with modern techniques, creating a productive and educational environment throughout the year.

    Vegetables & Seasonal Harvest

    • Leafy greens, herbs, and local vegetables grown for fresh community use.
    • Hands-on learning about compost, soil care, and sustainable planting.
    • Harvest sessions that connect children and adults to healthy food sources.

    Bees & Pollination

    • Bee zones that support pollination across our flowers and crops.
    • Awareness activities on the role of bees in biodiversity and food systems.
    • Guided observation to appreciate bee behavior in a safe way.

    Hydroponic Systems

    • Vertical hydroponics: stacked planting channels that maximize small spaces.
    • Horizontal hydroponics: broad-flow rows ideal for easy maintenance and harvest.
    • Water-efficient methods that demonstrate smart agriculture for urban communities.
