Our Animal Family

We care for a diverse group of friendly animals and use each habitat as an opportunity to teach compassion, responsibility, and respect for living things.

Rabbit

Gentle companions that help visitors learn calm handling and proper feeding routines.

Tortoise

Slow and steady residents that demonstrate long-term care and habitat awareness.

Hedgehog

Small nocturnal friends that spark curiosity about wildlife behavior and safe interaction.

Parrots

Colorful and vocal birds that engage visitors with sound, movement, and enrichment play.

Koi Fish

Elegant fish in our pond ecosystem, showing the balance of water quality and feeding care.

Guinea Pig

Social, adorable pets that are ideal for introducing children to gentle animal care.

Cats

Playful garden guardians that add warmth and companionship around the farm area.

Vegetables, Bees & Hydroponic Innovation

Tempua Kebun combines traditional gardening with modern techniques, creating a productive and educational environment throughout the year.

Vegetables & Seasonal Harvest

  • Leafy greens, herbs, and local vegetables grown for fresh community use.
  • Hands-on learning about compost, soil care, and sustainable planting.
  • Harvest sessions that connect children and adults to healthy food sources.

Bees & Pollination

  • Bee zones that support pollination across our flowers and crops.
  • Awareness activities on the role of bees in biodiversity and food systems.
  • Guided observation to appreciate bee behavior in a safe way.

Hydroponic Systems

  • Vertical hydroponics: stacked planting channels that maximize small spaces.
  • Horizontal hydroponics: broad-flow rows ideal for easy maintenance and harvest.
  • Water-efficient methods that demonstrate smart agriculture for urban communities.
